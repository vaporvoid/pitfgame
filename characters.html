Characters

Meet some of the characters you will come across along the way, as well as the main chracter.

Pumpkin

A Mongolian gerbil;

Pumpkin lives by himself in a large, castle-shaped cage; he has lived there ever since the day he was adopted from a local pet store. He loves to watch whatever comes on the TV outside his cage.

A lover of routine, Pumpkin tries his best to stay organized and to never disappoint the two humans who let his cage reside in their home.

Toodles

A Syrian hamster, or, "Fancy Hamster";

She means well, though her inaction, jealousy, and stubborn attitude lead to conflict. Her goal is to convince Pumpkin he'd be better off as a wild animal like her, mainly out of spite toward her own past.

Though her ambitions seem obvious at first, they are slowly unraveled as the game progresses...

Rapture

A raccoon;

He pretends to be scary, but he's too soft. He's a kind scavenger who teaches Pumpkin his ways and helps him create a stealthy little costume.

Rapture likes to keep shiny little things he finds in the trash and gifts them to his son, Clarence.

Enigma

A Great Horned Owl;

An ill-intending foe by the name of Enigma. Enigma is a firm believer in the food chain and lets his stomach speak for him. He's wise, but his stomach often gets in the way.

Enigma's backstory leads him to be the type of leader he is. He believes this order of ways will keep the forest from chaos and ultimately, avoiding past events from reoccurring, as he believes the events were caused by bad omens.
