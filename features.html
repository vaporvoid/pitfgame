Features


Make impactful choices

Choices that matter, branching and strategies that can evolve and expand as you play.

  • Choose who to befriend
  • Traverse cautiously
  • Optional quests
  • More replay-ability!

Suit up for strategy and hidden rewards

Receive outfits throughout the game, these outfits will help you complete certain puzzles, battles, and other events, as well as find hidden items.

Learn more about the outfits:
Main

Acorns as ammo (ranged attack).
Climb to travel and jump on top of enemies.

Scavenge for items easier and hold things firmly.
This costume makes Pumpkin stealthier and uses “clever” traps (trash) to stop/defeat opponents.

The main “attack” of the turtle costume is its defensive properties which will cause a ricochet effect.
Furthermore, the shell provides great general defense.

Post-game and hidden

The crow costume grants you the ability to fast travel across every area throughout the game.

The bat costume allows flight and utilizes echo location. The bat costume's attack is “bite”.
