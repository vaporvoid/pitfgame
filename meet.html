Meet The Team


Zac

Lead Creative Director

Creator of this website and the concept with which this game is based off.
In his free time, Zac enjoys writing, doodling, listening to music, and playing games. Some of his favorite game series include Pokemon, Pikmin, and Minecraft.

Pumpkin in the Forest is Zac's first solo game concept.

Contact: zac@pitf-thegame.com


Rhiannon

Concept Artist & User Testing

Rhiannon created most of the concept art for the site, specifically the character profiles seen on Characters (besides Enigma, that abomination was made by Zac). She also drew the banner seen on the home page (Zac added the sky part - blueness and clouds).

Rhiannon enjoys drawing, gaming, and hanging out with her cats. Some of her favorite game series include Fire Emblem, Pokemon, Destiny, and more. She absolutely loves Pumpkin in the Forest, it is her favorite game of all time.


Gavin

External Commentary & User Testing

Gavin helped with user testing; commenting on if links were working, the designs were cohesive, and the content being logically organized.
Gavin's favorite game series are Fallout, Forza, Minecraft, Call of Duty, and Battlefield.


Griffin

Lead of Finances & Budgeting

He is very good at his job. Without him, none of this would have been possible.
Griffin likes to spectate. He also likes to play hide & seek. His favorite franchise is Digimon. He also likes jazz.

Griffin is Rhiannon's stay-at-home cat when not busy with work.


Elroy

Lead of Snacks & Walks

Elroy likes to go on walks, bark at random things, and lounge on people furniture (furniture meant for humans, not made of humans). He is also a fan of the X-Files series. Elroy enjoys reading coffee table books.

Elroy works very hard to eat all of our snacks in an effort to make sure nothing goes to waste.


Bijou

Lead Shadow Chaser & General Security

No shadows get past Bijou, she is very good at what she does. She likes to watch cooking shows on TV and is especially fond of Cutthroat Kitchen.


Merlin

General Counsel

Merlin is a top tier, high-level consultant of the bar and per legal reasons, he is actually also a dog and doesn't actually have any licenses of any kind. He likes treats and lounging on human furniture (again, furniture for humans, not furniture made from humans).

Merlin likes true crime podcasts & documentaries.


Bailey

Positive Reinforcement & User Testing

Bailey helps to say positive things about the website, game concept, and the staff here at our beautiful little studio. Spirits never fall low with Bailey's endless stories about cats and other old lady things.

Her favorite games are Sims and the Desperate Housewives PC game.
