Learn about the studio behind the game

Established by Zac and Rhiannon in St. Louis, Missouri, Leaful Creations is the studio behind Pumpkin in the Forest, as well as Our Short-term Youth, The Caves of Darkness, and Goop Escape.

Though the games made and shared by Leaful Creations are made entirely by Zachary and Rhiannon, we do have a team of helpers who provide consistent feedback and help us stay motivated! You can meet them over on Meet the Team (as well as some info about Zac and Rhiannon).
