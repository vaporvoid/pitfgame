Behind the scenes of Pumpkin in the Forest

A lot had to go into the making of Pumpkin in the Forest, from concept art, writing stories, exploring the characters and the region, and more. Below, you can read quite a bit about this process.

If you like the content below, all of it and then a lot more is featured in our Pumpkin in the Forest Art Concept Book, available on our shop.

I. Welcome to Turnabout Forest

This map is a loose concept of Turnabout Forest in its early stages from an aerial view.


The map features the neighborhood from which Pumpkin resides, the Talkabout Tree, the Depths of Turnabout, and the creek. The map also features some of the other areas featured in the game, including the abandoned barn and the pumpkin prairie fields. This is not all of the areas, as it was an early concept.
